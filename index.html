ABOUT THE METROCLUB

For those seeking a luxurious, comfortable, urban retreat in the heart of Philadelphia's Olde City, the metroclub condominiums is home.

The MetroClub condominiums at 8th and Race Streets offers Center City convenience while providing easy access to major highways. Luxury and spacious, one and two bedroom residences and penthouses. With 9-foot, 11-foot, 12-foot ceilings fill the renovated Metropolitan Hospital. The original white marble walls and terrazzo flooring in the lobby were incorporated into the renovations and provide a reminder of the building's history. The distinctive curved facade is wrapped in rose-colored glass; Full windows will give most residences incredible views of either center city or the ben franklin bridge.
